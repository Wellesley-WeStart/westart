about us

WeStart is Wellesley College's entrepreneurship club. We strive to foster an entrepreneurial spirit on our campus. From bringing successful entrepreneurs on campus to hosting Wellesley's first Pitch Competition, we aim to expose students to the exciting world of entrepreneurship.
Below, you can find some events hosted by the club.

P

Pitch Competition

WeStart, in collarboration with WWIB organized Wellesley's first Start Up Pitch Competition, with prize money worth $5000. With over 15 teams and 40 partipants, the competition was a huge success. WeStart also organized a lecture series prior to the competition to help participants develop their business plans and think about financing.

T

Career Talks

WeStart hosted various talks to expose the community to alternative career paths in entrepreneurship and tech. We brought in individuals with experience as product managers in high-growth start ups and as Angel Investors for a lecture series as well as representatives from Draper University, a start-up bootcamp.

L

Lecture Series

WeStart hosted a lecture series with notable alumni in the Boston start-up scene such as C.A. Webb and Sarah Blake Case. Students were exposed to everything from workshops on how to start a business to learning about venture capital.
